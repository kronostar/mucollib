    MuColLib

    A database backed tool geared at providing a comprehensive resource of information about a music collection. This goes way beyond just recording the Artist and Album title, although it can do that too!

    What you can store:

    • Artist
    • Albums by Artist/Storage format/Genre/Record label
    • Store catalogue numbers, release dates
    • Songs by Album/Composer
    • Musicians by Album/Song
    • Extensive support for songs in sections or movements
